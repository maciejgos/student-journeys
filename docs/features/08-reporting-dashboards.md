# Reporting and Dashboards

## Description

As a team lead or administrator, I need dashboards and reports so that workload, pipeline health, service performance, and retention risk can be monitored.

## Acceptance criteria

- The system provides reports for students by journey stage, pipeline volumes, outstanding tasks, open cases, risk flags, average case resolution time, status movement trends, and withdrawals or deferments by reason.
- Reports support filtering by campus, program, date range, and owner.
- Dashboards present summary information appropriate to team lead and administrator roles.
- Reporting data reflects the latest available operational data for the selected filters.
