# Alerts and Risk Flags

## Description

As a backoffice worker or team lead, I need the system to surface missing actions and student risk signals so that intervention happens before deadlines or service issues are missed.

## Acceptance criteria

- The system supports configurable alerts for missing required documents, upcoming deadlines, no recent engagement, repeated failed contact attempts, tuition or payment issues, and attendance or academic risk signals.
- Authorized users can apply a manual risk flag with a reason.
- Authorized users can remove a manual risk flag with a reason or resolution note.
- Active alerts and risk flags are visible on the student record and in relevant queue views.
